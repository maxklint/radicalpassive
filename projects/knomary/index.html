Knomary office

The starting point for our design was the initially complex configuration of the space, the wide range of wall materials, variations in ceiling height and floor level. To create a comfortable environment, we decided to tie the existing surfaces together with a common light grey colour, against which the black pavilion with private offices and a call booth stands out. This way we were free to introduce a variety of complementary materials and shapes of furniture: stainless steel, light wood, red textile. As much as possible, workspaces are placed along the windows. Two meeting rooms create contrasting zones: the first one for teamwork and lectures, the second one for important business meetings. The common space with upholstered furniture and stairs is there to add a casualness to the overall atmosphere.

Project year: 2021
Area: 250 square meters
Status: Concept
In collaboration with Lisa Shirochina
