Elemental bedside cabinet

It is a pure geometric object. The inconspicuous drawers are opened by push. The volume is large enough to contain the bedtime necessities. This bedside table is made of solid oak, coated in wood stain. Our design inspiration: the black monolith from Kubrick’s Space Odyssey. Seen in Melancholy space.

Available for order.
