Radical Passive is an architectural group formed in 2019 with offices in Minsk and Brussels. We create new architecture of public spaces, buildings, interiors and furniture elements, we develop projects of renovation and reconstruction. Our works are free to speak their own language, without excess meaning but radically aimed at evoking feelings.
