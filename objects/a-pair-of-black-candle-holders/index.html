A pair of black candle holders

We have a tradition of dedicating a candle holder to each client that we work with and giving it to them as a present at the end of the project. So, the object is designed with a particular person or couple in mind and can be placed in the interior that we designed for them.

This pair of black candle holders was designed for a young couple living in Grey apartment. The couple met each other, started living together and even got married during the project. So to celebrate them and the other couples, we came up with two candleholders that complement each other but remain different.
