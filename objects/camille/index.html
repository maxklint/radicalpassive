Camille

A big enveloping cloud. It’s bigger than a normal chair: its dimensions defy common standards. You don’t sit on this chair, you stay in it. Furthermore, sitting in the chair is just one of the possible poses. Its soft, rounded shape gives it a relaxed and stable character. The upholstery is made of white textured corduroy.

The chair is inspired by the works of the French studio Pierre Augustin Rose and the Russian designer Olga Engel.
