Auguste

The chair is on the border between a functional object and a sculpture. It was specifically designed to stand out in the interior. It has no backrest, making it more of a cushioned stool. The armrests flow smoothly from the shape of the seat. The solid base gives the entire structure stability. The distinctive shape is accentuated by the black velour upholstery.

The chair is inspired by the works of the French studio Pierre Augustin Rose and the Russian designer Olga Engel.

Available for order. Сolour can be changed upon request.
